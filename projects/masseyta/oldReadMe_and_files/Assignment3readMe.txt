To run program:

make unittestresults.out
cat unittestresults.out

make unittestresults.out will make all the test cases, and execute the functions, in addition to storing results and coverage.
The top of the unittestresults.out will be the printout of all functions, pass and fail results.
The bottom of the unittestresults.out will be the coverage of each function in dominion.c, plus total coverage for dominion.c
